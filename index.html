    Simple Ranking Tool





    About

    This is a website I wrote up to help me rank arbitrary things. You start by posing a comparison question (ex. "Which one do you prefer?", "Which one sounds better to you?" etc.) and then you write down a list of items you want to compete against each other. Submit the form when you're ready, and then vote on each randomized pair you are presented with. Once you have voted enough times, the rankings should be accurate enough to determine your personal preferences.
